# Matthew Mettler

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. My research focuses on American political behavior, public opinion, misinformation, democratic accountability, and political methodology.

This GitHub profile hosts replication materials, analysis scripts, and project documentation for my research.

## Research Areas

- Political behavior and public opinion
- Fact-opinion differentiation and misinformation
- Retrospective voting and democratic accountability
- Survey and experimental methods
- Text analysis and political communication

## Selected Projects

- **Fact-Opinion Differentiation**  
  Replication materials and analysis scripts for research on citizens’ classification of factual and opinion-based statements.

- **Retrospective Voting and Political Memory**  
  Materials for projects on memory of political performance, sociotropic evaluations, and vote choice.

- **Political Rhetoric and Personalization**  
  Text-analysis materials related to personalization, self-reference, and political communication.
